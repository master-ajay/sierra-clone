# Shipping Policy

We ship all orders within 2 business days of purchase. Standard shipping
takes 5-7 business days to arrive within the continental United States.
Expedited shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee and
takes 2-3 business days.

International orders may take 10-20 business days depending on customs
processing in the destination country. We are not responsible for customs
fees or import duties charged by the destination country.

If your order has not arrived within the expected window, contact support
with your order number so we can investigate with the carrier.
