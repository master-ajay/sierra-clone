# Account and Password FAQ

To reset your password, go to the sign-in page and click "Forgot
password." Enter the email address associated with your account and we
will send a reset link that is valid for 24 hours.

If you no longer have access to the email on file, contact support to
verify your identity and update your account email before resetting your
password.

You can change your email address, shipping addresses, and notification
preferences from the Account Settings page once signed in.
